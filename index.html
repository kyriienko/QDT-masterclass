About the Masterclass

Discover the latest advancements in Quantum Digital Twin technology and its applications across various industries.

The Quantum Digital Twin Masterclass is a four-day intensive program designed for professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts interested in the intersection of quantum computing and digital twin technology. Through a series of lectures, workshops, and hands-on sessions, participants will gain deep insights into creating, managing, and optimizing digital twins using quantum technologies.
